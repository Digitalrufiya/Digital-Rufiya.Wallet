What is the Immune System?

The immune system is the body's defense mechanism against harmful pathogens such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, and parasites. It is a complex network of cells, tissues, and organs that work together to protect the body from infections and maintain overall health.

Diseases / Disorders

Some common immune system disorders include:

  • Autoimmune diseases (e.g., rheumatoid arthritis, lupus)
  • Immunodeficiency disorders (e.g., primary immunodeficiency, HIV)
  • Allergies and hypersensitivities
