Description

This natural black sapphire exhibits a deep black color with a mirror-like polished surface. Internal needle-like inclusions, characteristic of corundum minerals, are visible under magnification, confirming its natural origin. The stone demonstrates excellent hardness, approximately 9 on the Mohs scale, resisting scratches from glass, nails, and knives under normal conditions. This sapphire does not register on a standard diamond tester, consistent with its mineral properties distinct from diamond.
