My Gemstone Discovery Journey

Spiritual Beginning

My journey began with a powerful dream. In it, I was asked to recite the Dua of Arafat three times. A voice asked me three times what I wanted, and I replied, "To pray in front of the Kaaba." Then I was shown a black diamond and white diamond. The voice said, "Soon, you will enter Makkah for prayer. You are the richest person in the universe." That same day, I discovered rare black and white minerals on a Maldivian beach.

Spiritual Knowledge & Skills

Without studying from gemology books or labs, I was spiritually guided to understand the names, properties, and identities of minerals. I could recognize them instantly. I began polishing and cutting gemstones using simple tools — without any formal training. This path reminded me of the wisdom granted to prophets like Solomon and David (peace be upon them).

Divine Journey to Makkah

Three years later, I was blessed with a free trip to Makkah — all costs covered by a company. It confirmed my dream and spiritual path. I realized my responsibility: to use these gifts to help others, share knowledge, and build a transparent gem discovery initiative.

Gemstone Gallery

Purpose & Vision

My mission is to certify, share, and sell natural gemstones to support orphans, poor communities, and Islamic educational projects. I also plan to build a transparent learning center to teach others how to recognize and value gemstones — based on internal guidance, faith, and experience.

Disclaimer: This story is shared to document my spiritual and personal journey. All discoveries are verified through physical and internal evaluation, not formal laboratory testing. Please conduct your own analysis for legal or commercial use.
